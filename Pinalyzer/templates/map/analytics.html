What does the score mean ?

The score rates someone's influence and popularity on Pinterest. The higher someone's score is, the more influential he or she is. The minimum score is 0 and the highest score possible is 100.

How does Pinalyzer compute my score ?

Pinalyzer evaluates how people are interested in your pins. Essentially, we look at how many followers you have and how many people like, repin and comment your pins.
